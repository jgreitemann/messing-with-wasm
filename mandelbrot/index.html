Hints:

  • Change color palette used for rendering from the drop down. All color palettes have been adapted from ColorBrewer.
  • Left-click-drag a zoom rectange, release to zoom-in. You can return to your previous zoom level with the Undo button, or reset the zoom to capture the full apple man.
  • The calculation of the fractal happens client-side in your browser using WebAssembly. While the calculation is in progress, a spinner animation is shown.
